# THE SPONGE · SPN

> A sphere of **UD0** — the ROOT0 universe / biosphere. Domain: **LIFE SCIENCE**.

THE SPONGE (SPN) — the other floor of animal life, beside Trichoplax: an animal with NO neurons, muscles, organs, or symmetry — a colony of cells pumping water through a living filter (collar cells / choanocytes). Yet it behaves: it contracts in a slow whole-body 'SNEEZE' to flush clogged pores, coordinated by chemical/electrical cell-to-cell signaling with no nerves; and (Wilson 1907) pushed through a sieve into single cells, the cells crawl back together and REBUILD the animal. Carries the proto-synaptic gene toolkit (a nervous system's parts, unassembled); its collar cell ≈ the choanoflagellate at the origin of all animals. LIVE filter-and-sneeze interactive. Two-layer honest; H. V. Wilson (d.1939) minted, living researchers (Leys et al.) cited. 10 emergents.
